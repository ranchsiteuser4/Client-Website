ALL Gifts are 100% tax deductible; we are a 501c3 non-profit U.S. Organization.


- SEED MONEY: As Restoration and Hope begins its initial launch, seed money will be necessary to defray many of the costs related to starting such a program from scratch--this general fund would help to cover the expenses of not only physically running retreats, (cost of renting a conference room, materials for the retreat, etc.) including promotional & marketing materials, such as: paper, envelopes, stamps, printer ink, but would also help with a variety of other needs, allowing the retreats to run smoothly and effectively.

- CAPITAL GIFTS: Currently we are in a building campaign and all funds will be used for the development and construction of the retreat ranch, which includes employee support.

- LIFE SUPPORT PROGRAM: This monthly check donation will allow us to focus on the building of the Retreat ranch, and to give 100% of our time to the program and impacting others.

- ANNUAL LOVE SPONSOR: This annual donation is for families who have lost a child and understand the pain others are experiencing. This yearly gift of $1,000 or more is given in the memory and name of the child they lost.
