"An amazing Angel who came into my life in a time of loss. Ione has helped me see so many things and I now have clarity." -J.S., Wenatchee, WA

"On August 2013, I lost my son in a car accident. I thought I was going crazy, my pain was so unbearable. So, by October when I was searching online for help, I ran into the website for Restoration and Hope Ranch. I got in contact with Ione Harris and she emailed me back right away. Since we live 5-6 hours away from each other, we set up weekly phone meetings and she started coaching me, guiding me through my new Journey, she sent me a workbook and for a year we worked together. There were times when I would be at work and I got so emotional, but Ione Harris was just a phone call away. Ione helped me understand that all that I was feeling was normal, a part of grieving, that I had to learn to accept this new life without my son and with God's help. Ione understood exactly what I was going through since she also had a loss. Ione Harris has helped me understand that God was still on my side, to let God take my pain and let God take care of my loss and my future. I was not alone anymore. Ione became a good friend and is a part of my life. I think God put her in my path as my guardian angel when I most needed one. Thank you!" - L.M., Clear Lake, CA
