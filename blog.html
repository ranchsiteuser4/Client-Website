Over the years, it has been my joy to work with parents on a one to one basis. Our sessions together have been weekly by phone, using a workbook that takes them through the grieving process. I see them growing, healing and becoming transformed from the hurt and brokenness of losing a child. It's still a journey for them, and will be; but the growth, awareness and understanding that comes through their grief work is quite rewarding to see. I am truly Blessed to work with such wonderful Mothers.
